Alcohol
A legal but addictive depressant, widely consumed in Kerala. Often a gateway to dependence.
6-12 months regular use can lead to dependency
Risks:
  • Liver failure, heart disease, cancer
  • Increased risk of road accidents
  • Family & social problems, domestic violence
  • Legal consequences (drunk driving, underage)
